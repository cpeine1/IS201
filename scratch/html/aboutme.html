About Me

I am a motivated Pre-Business student at Brigham Young University, specializing in finance and business management. With a GPA of 3.99 and multiple scholarships, I’ve built a solid foundation in finance, economics, and accounting. I take pride in my leadership abilities, currently serving as an Infield Mentor at the Missionary Training Center, where I support new missionaries, lead training sessions, and help assess progress in a diverse, multicultural environment.

As the co-founder of The Green Thumb Guys, a residential landscaping business, I’ve managed client relationships, overseen multiple projects, and developed marketing strategies that fueled significant growth. Additionally, my experience as a private contractor for Inside Outside Home Solutions allowed me to expand my technical skill set in home remodeling and construction, consistently achieving high customer satisfaction.

Fluent in Spanish, I have a strong passion for working with diverse groups, whether it’s teaching English to non-native speakers or collaborating with international teams. I’m always open to connecting with professionals in business and finance and am excited to explore opportunities in leadership, strategy, and entrepreneurship.
